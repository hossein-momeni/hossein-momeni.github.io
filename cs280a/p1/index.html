Project 0: Becoming Friends with Your Camera — My Results

Course: CS280a — Intro to Computer Vision & Computational Photography · Author: Hossein Momeni

Part 1: Selfie — Wrong vs. Right

Close up wide angle vs step-back + zoom for the same face size.

Observation

With increased subject distance and a longer focal length, rays approach parallel for facial features.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

Zoomed-in from afar vs moved-forward no-zoom to match framing.

Observation

Changing camera position (center of projection) changes perspective. Focal length crops/magnifies: perspective flattening comes from shooting farther away, not from focal length alone.

Part 3: The Dolly Zoom

Simultaneous dolly back (increase distance) and zoom in (increase focal length) to approximately preserve subject size while the background appears to expand/contract.
